Sifr is the Arabic root of the word zero. From zero, everything is possible. We built Sifr Collection so the people deploying capital in MENA carbon can finally see their own market clearly.

The global data houses treat the Middle East as a footnote. We treat it as the whole product. Sifr Collection is the independent intelligence platform for carbon across the Middle East and North Africa.

What we do

We bring the MENA carbon market into one place. Reference prices, a live project tracker, policy and scheme intelligence, country profiles, and soil and geospatial data. The aim is simple: be the first thing a buyer, a developer or a ministry reads on regional carbon, and be right every time.

Why independence matters

We do not broker credits, rate them for a fee, or run a trading venue. We are an information platform, and that is the point. A government or a cautious corporate can trust a neutral regional source in a way they cannot trust a vendor with a position to sell. Our job is to tell you what the market is doing, not to sell you a place in it.

How we are different

Sylvera and BeZero tell the world whether a credit is good. AlliedOffsets tells the world what the market did. Sifr tells the Middle East what its own market is doing, with the regional depth, the language, and the access that a London ratings agency cannot match. We track the Gulf CCS giants the pure-credit players ignore, and the local schemes that actually move a regional buyer.

Built on public data

Our data comes from public carbon registries, exchange and compliance markets, national submissions and primary sources, refreshed daily and dated on the page. We document our methodology openly so the numbers can be checked. No black box, no paywalled mystery index.

Who we serve

Governments

Ministries and sovereign bodies building national carbon infrastructure, who need an independent read on the region they can trust.

Corporates

Sustainability and procurement leads sizing a credit budget or a regional strategy, who need real prices and credible projects.

Developers

Project developers and verifiers benchmarking against the regional market and tracking the schemes that govern issuance.

Where we are

Founded in 2026 and based in Dubai, Sifr Collection is part of the Aston Group. We work with corporates, project developers and government bodies across the region. For partnership, data access, or a briefing, get in touch.
